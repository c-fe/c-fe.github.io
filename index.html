Learning and Cultural Exchange Centre for the Expat Community in Teulada (Spain)

 

Fun & Culture offers its customers many Spanish learning opportunities. 

 

 

We have Spanish language courses for beginners to advanced, giving Spanish lessons to all age groups.

 

 

 

As well as learning the Spanish Language you can also learn many interesting facts about Spanish Culture.

 

 

Our centre is based in Teulada, we welcome clients from Denia, Javea, Moraira, Benitachell, Benissa and many other locations on the Costa Blanca.

Our courses are available under three group headings:

  • Cultural and Language Courses
  • Computer and Internet Courses
  • Personal Development Courses
